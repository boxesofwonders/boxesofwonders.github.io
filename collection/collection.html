The collection

Each Item is an unique combination of box type, status and contents. The name and the story personalize the atmosphere. Some hidden quantity add a veil of mystery. The result is a special treasure made to stay forever.

(Any resemblance to real events and/or to real persons is purely coincidental)




Boxes

Wood Box

It's the cheapest one, easy to hide but fragile.

Iron Box

It has the best ferromagnetic properties but rusts in humid air.

Bronze Box

It's the best conductor of electricity, resistant to corrosion but it's not ductile.

Aluminum Box

It's the most resistant to frost, it can be deformed but it does not fracture.

Steel Box

It has the best hardness, good thermal conductivity, stainless.

Titanium Box

It's light weight, strong, ductile, shiny, resistant to corrosion, hard to melt, acid resistant.




Status

Opened

The content is known and visible to all. They rarely have secret contents.

Closed

They are a mystery. Only their owner can access the secret contents.
